Medieval Rule

Roles

King

"Ruler of all, as King your word is final."

Ability: +3 Votes

Priest

"Blessed father, as Priest you purify the unholy."

Ability: Block another player's ability

Apothecary

"Savior of all, as Apothecary you save lives."

Ability: Choose a player to prevent them from dying this turn

Watchman

"A night wanderer, as Watchman you're the people's eyes."

Ability: See which player visits another player at night

Warden

"People demand justice, as Warden you are justice."

Ability: In the morning you send a player to jail, at night you may execute them

Knight

"Honor in battle, as Knight you're the peoples sword and shield."

Ability: Pick a player to guard at night, if any other player tries to kill that guarded player, kill the killer but you die guarding them.

Peeler

"No one is innocent, as Peeler you seek the truth."

Ability: Evil players come up as "Suspect" as well as "Framed" players, except Cult leaders.

Archer

"Pierce all evil, as Archer you strike down foes."

Ability: Choose a player to kill at night, if they're innocent the next day you die from guilt

Assassin

"The dark knight, as Assassin you kill for the cult."

Ability: Kill a player at night chosen by the Cult Leader, if the Cult Leader is "Jailed", you choose who to kill

Framer

"A miscreant, as Framer you mislead the town."

Ability: "Frame" another player as evil

Cult Leader

"The root of all evil, as Cult Leader you command death."

Ability: Choose who the assassin kills at night

Mad Hatter

"Insanity Incarnate, as Mad Hatter you want death to all."

Ability: Choose who to kill at night
